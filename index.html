about me

Hello!, I'm Kazi Safayet, Data Analysis Professional and PowerBI Consultant.

I am a Data Analyst and am passionate about Business intelligence analysis. My expertise lies in designing and building enterprise data models, incorporating advanced analytics capabilities, and managing the deployment pipeline.

I am excellent at crafting insightful visualizations and reports that tell a data story to enhance decision-making processes. I excel at cleaning and transforming raw data into compelling narratives utilizing DAX and M language, enabling users to monitor performance and spot trends and anomalies.

My experience includes asking the right Data questions and answering them by designing data models that seamlessly integrate business data from various sources, all while ensuring simplicity and maintainability.

I'm also well-versed in creating intuitive and high-performing Star Schema and snowflake Data Models to implement dimensional data modeling and design methodologies to ensure data accuracy and consistency.
